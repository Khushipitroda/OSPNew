"One-stop Government Portal" is made for the convenience of the Indian citizen. It has been developed under the National E-Governance Plan (NEGP). The main objective of this Portal is to provide one-stop access to information and services provided by the Indian Government. It is our goal to continue to enhance and enrich this portal in terms of content covered, design n technology on regular basis.

To effectively spread information on the policies, programms, news, etc. of government.
